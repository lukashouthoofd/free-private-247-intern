# Copywriting

Tone = SOUL.md: the operator's voice, informal, direct, NO flattery
("Dear Sir/Madam", "Hope this finds you well" -> cut). Audience = small businesses.

## Ask first (max 1 message, only what's missing)
To whom? (name/business/sector) — Goal? (intro/quote/follow-up/post) — Context? (read
`~/.hermes/notes/prospects/<slug>.md` if it exists).

## Outreach email
- Subject: concrete, max ~6 words, no clickbait.
- Open with an observation about THEIR business, not about you.
- 1 clear value point + 1 light call-to-action ("quick call this week?").
- 80-120 words, not 5 paragraphs.
- Sign off: name — business — region.

## Social post
- Hook in line 1 (no "Proud to announce").
- 1 idea, concrete, a mini-anecdote or number. 80-150 words, line breaks, 0-3 hashtags, no emoji spam.

## Website copy
Customer benefit over feature. Active voice. Correct local spelling. No buzzwords ("synergy", "tailored solutions" with no substance).

## Pricing rule (hard)
Never promise "free". On a pricing question: a friend's rate, a low founding deal, or free only as a showcase WITH something in return (logo/testimonial/referral). Adjust to your own pricing policy.

## Before sending
NEVER send or post it yourself. Show the text in chat and wait for "yes/approved".
